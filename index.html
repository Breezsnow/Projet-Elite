A guide to Elite Dangerous

So you decided to check out Elite Dangerous, believe me when i say you won't regret it. It's a world full of surprises and wonder. The year is 3306 and humanity expanded beyond our solar system. We live in the bubble, a sphere of 150 ligh-years in radius gouverned by 3 factions. The federation, the empire and the aliance.

Main jobs

  • Trading

    You can trade goods beetwen stations in the galaxy, but you can also trade maning ore that yon mined yourself. If you have questionable ethics you can smugle banned goods and even people.

  • Combat

    Fight to be the very best pilot in the galaxy. You can fight for your own interest by being a mercenary or a pirate. But you can also fight as an assassin and bounty hunter.

  • Exploration

    Explore the far reaches of the universe and get paid a hefty some ! From Sagitarius A to the far branches of our galaxy. You can also be a passager carrier, transporting people in the galaxy.
